## Getting Started

### Registration
1. Navigate to the registration page
2. Enter your email, username, and password
3. Click "Register" to create your account

### Login
1. Navigate to the login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Login" to access your account

## Creating Characters

Characters are your role-playing personas in OwlQuill. You can create multiple characters to use in different realms and stories.

### To Create a Character:
1. Navigate to the "Characters" page from the sidebar
2. Click "Create Character"
3. Fill in character details:
   - **Name**: Your character's name (required)
   - **Alias**: Alternative name or nickname
   - **Species**: What kind of being they are (human, elf, dragon, etc.)
   - **Age**: Character's age
   - **Role**: Their role or occupation
   - **Era**: Time period they're from
   - **Bio**: Short and long descriptions of your character
   - **Avatar**: Profile picture
   - **Tags**: Keywords to describe your character
   - **Visibility**: Who can see your character (public, friends, private)
4. Click "Save" to create your character

### Using AI Assistance
The "Generate with AI" feature can help you create character bios based on the basic information you provide.

## Joining Realms

Realms are communities where role-play takes place. Each realm has its own theme, genre, and rules.

### To Join a Realm:
1. Navigate to the "Realms" page
2. Browse available realms or use the search function
3. Click on a realm to view details
4. Click "Join Realm" to become a member

### Creating a Realm:
1. From the Realms page, click "Create Realm"
2. Fill in realm details:
   - **Name**: Realm name (required)
   - **Slug**: URL-friendly identifier
   - **Tagline**: Short description
   - **Description**: Detailed information about the realm
   - **Genre**: The realm's genre or theme
   - **Visibility**: Public or private
3. Click "Create" to establish your realm

## Creating Posts and Scenes

Posts are the heart of storytelling in OwlQuill. There are three types of posts:

### Post Types:
- **IC (In-Character)**: Posts written from your character's perspective
- **OOC (Out-of-Character)**: Meta discussions and planning
- **Narration**: Scene-setting and environmental descriptions

### To Create a Post:
1. Navigate to a realm you've joined
2. Click "Create Post"
3. Select a character (for IC posts)
4. Choose the post type
5. Write your content
6. Optionally add a title
7. Click "Post" to publish

### Interacting with Posts:
- **Comment**: Add your response or continue the story
- **React**: Show appreciation with emoji reactions

## Using Direct Messages

Direct Messages (DMs) allow you to have private conversations with other users.

### Starting a Conversation:
1. Navigate to the "Messages" page from the sidebar
2. Click the "New" button in the top-right
3. Enter the username of the person you want to message
4. Click "Start Conversation"
5. Type your message and click "Send"

### Managing Conversations:
- The conversation list shows all your active chats
- Unread messages are indicated with a badge showing the count
- Click on a conversation to view and respond to messages
- Messages are automatically marked as read when you open a conversation

### Message Features:
- **Real-time Updates**: While not true real-time (no websockets yet), conversations update when you send or receive messages
- **Message History**: All your message history is preserved
- **Unread Indicators**: See at a glance which conversations have new messages

### Tips for Using DMs:
- Use DMs for one-on-one planning and coordination
- Keep realm-specific discussions in realm posts for transparency
- Be respectful and follow community guidelines in private messages

## Profile Management

### Updating Your Profile:
1. Navigate to the "Profile" page
2. Update your information:
   - **Display Name**: Your preferred display name
   - **Bio**: Tell others about yourself
   - **Avatar**: Profile picture URL
3. Click "Save Changes"

### Logging Out:
Click the "Logout" button at the bottom of the sidebar to sign out of your account.

## Tips and Best Practices

1. **Create Diverse Characters**: Don't be afraid to experiment with different character types
2. **Respect Realm Themes**: Follow the genre and rules of each realm you join
3. **Engage with Others**: Comment on posts and collaborate on stories
4. **Use Direct Messages**: Coordinate complex storylines privately before posting
5. **Be Creative**: OwlQuill is your canvas for collaborative storytelling
