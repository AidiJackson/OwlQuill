# OwlQuill User Guide

## Welcome to OwlQuill

OwlQuill is a roleplay-focused social network where you can create characters, join realms (communities), participate in scenes (roleplay threads), and interact with other roleplayers.

## Getting Started

### 1. Create an Account
- Visit the registration page
- Provide your email, username, and password
- You're ready to start!

### 2. Create Your First Character
- Go to the **Characters** page
- Click **Create Character**
- Fill in the details:
  - **Name**: Your character's name
  - **Species**: Human, elf, dragon, etc.
  - **Role**: Warrior, mage, merchant, etc.
  - **Era**: Modern, medieval, futuristic, etc.
  - **Bio**: Short and long bios to describe your character
  - **Portrait**: Optional image URL
  - **Tags**: Keywords for searchability
  - **Visibility**: Public, friends-only, or private

### 3. Join a Realm
- Go to the **Realms** page
- Browse the **Discover Realms** section
- Click **Join** on any public realm that interests you
- Your joined realms appear in **Your Realms**

### 4. Or Create Your Own Realm
- Click **Create Realm** on the Realms page
- Fill in the details:
  - **Name**: The realm's name
  - **Slug**: URL-friendly identifier (e.g., "fantasy-tavern")
  - **Tagline**: A catchy one-liner
  - **Description**: What's the realm about?
  - **Genre**: Fantasy, sci-fi, modern, etc.
  - **Banner**: Optional image URL
  - **Public/Private**: Control who can join

## Roleplaying

### Understanding Scenes
A **Scene** is a roleplay thread within a Realm. Think of it as a specific story or interaction happening in that world.

Example scenes:
- "The Dark Forest" - Characters exploring a mysterious forest
- "The Grand Ball" - A formal social event
- "The Tavern Brawl" - An action scene

### Creating a Scene
1. Navigate to a Realm you're a member of
2. Click **+ New Scene**
3. Provide a title and description
4. The scene is now ready for posts!

### Posting in a Scene
1. Enter a scene from the Realm page
2. Click **+ New Post**
3. Write your content
4. Choose a post type:
   - **IC (In-Character)**: Roleplay as your character
   - **OOC (Out-of-Character)**: Player commentary
   - **Narration**: Scene-setting or storytelling
5. Optionally select which character is posting (for IC posts)
6. Click **Post**

### Post Type Examples

**IC (In-Character) Post**:
```
*Luna steps into the tavern, her cloak dripping with rain*
"Anyone seen a merchant around here? I've got business to discuss."
```

**OOC Post**:
```
Hey everyone! I'll be away this weekend, so Luna won't be responding
until Monday. Feel free to continue without me!
```

**Narration Post**:
```
The storm outside grows fiercer. Thunder rumbles, shaking the windows
of the old tavern. The fireplace struggles to stay lit as wind howls
through the cracks in the walls.
```

## The Feed

Your **Home Feed** shows recent posts from all the realms you're a member of. It's a great way to keep up with ongoing stories and scenes across your communities.

## Profile Customization

Visit your **Profile** page to:
- Set your display name
- Add a bio
- Upload an avatar
- View your posting history

## Tips for Great Roleplay

1. **Be Descriptive**: Paint a picture with your words
2. **Respect Others**: Give everyone a chance to respond
3. **Stay In Character**: Use IC posts for character actions/dialogue
4. **Use OOC Wisely**: Coordinate with other players when needed
5. **Set the Scene**: Narration posts help build atmosphere
6. **Be Consistent**: Develop your character over time

## Community Guidelines

- **Be Respectful**: Treat all players with kindness
- **Collaborate**: Roleplay is cooperative storytelling
- **Communicate**: Use OOC posts to clarify or coordinate
- **Have Fun**: This is a creative space for imagination!

## Troubleshooting

### Can't post in a scene?
Make sure you're a member of the realm that scene belongs to.

### Can't create a scene?
You must be a member of a realm to create scenes in it.

### Post not showing up?
Check that you selected the correct scene and that your content was saved.

### Lost your password?
Contact the system administrator (password reset feature coming soon).

## Need Help?

- Reach out to your realm administrators
